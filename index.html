Popular in India

Beaches:

  • Havelock Island, Andaman & Nicobar Islands: Pristine beaches, coral reefs, rainforests.
  • Gokarna, Karnataka: Laid-back atmosphere, yoga retreats, charming cafes.
  • Varkala, Kerala: Clifftop cafes, Ayurvedic treatments, serene beaches.

History:

  • Hampi, Karnataka: Vijayanagara Empire ruins, UNESCO World Heritage site.
  • Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh: Holy city, rich in Hindu culture and spirituality.
  • Meenakshi Temple, Madurai: Dravidian architectural marvel, historical significance.

Nature:

  • Ladakh, Jammu & Kashmir: Majestic mountains, Buddhist monasteries, unique landscapes.
  • Munnar, Kerala: Rolling hills, tea plantations, and enchanting waterfalls.
  • Coorg, Karnataka: Coffee region, lush hills, abundant wildlife.

Off-the-Beaten-Path:

  • Mawlynnong, Meghalaya: "Cleanest village in India," an offbeat gem.
  • Ziro Valley, Arunachal Pradesh: Tranquil rice paddies, tribal villages, cultural richness.
  • Dzukou Valley, Nagaland: Carpeted with wildflowers, stunning mountain views.

Bonus Tips:

  • Best travel time: October-March (cool & dry), summer for hill stations.
  • Budget: Varies by travel style; affordable options available.
  • Tailor your trip: Customize based on your interests—history, nature, beaches, etc.
